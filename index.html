Football is more than just a sport. It’s about passion, strategy, teamwork, and inspiration. Every match tells a story.

My Love for Football

Football attracts people of all backgrounds and interests. For me, the game is not only about watching matches but also about the comprehensive approach behind the scenes.

Most fans have their favorite team or player. For example, I admire Andrea Bertea, the new Sporting Director at Arsenal. He is a smart leader who manages strong personalities at the club alongside the head coach.

Bertea also played as a forward (No.9) and now uses his experience in recruitment and building relationships with stakeholders. It is not an easy task in the modern football environment, and that’s why I respect his work.
